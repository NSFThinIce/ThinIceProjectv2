## Biography

Sophia Weg is an environmental biology major at SUNY New Paltz, working in Dr. Richardson's aquatic ecology lab. She helps with monthly Thin Ice sampling and processing at Mohonk Lake in the Hudson Valley

## Research Focus & Contributions

With Dr. Richardson’s assistance, She worked on her own project analyzing water clarity data to find seasonal and annual patterns and make connections with precipitation and phytoplankton abundance.
