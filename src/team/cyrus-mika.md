## Biography
Cyrus Mika is an alumni of SUNY New Paltz that majored in Environmental Studies with a double minor in Geology and Computer Science. She has formerly been active as a researcher in Dr. Richardson's Lab, assisting with field work and data collection from Mohonk Lake. 

## Research Focus
During the Summer of 2025, she worked on a project called "Classifying reservoirs into different types: does water body type affect responses to climate?" which was presented at the Ecological Society of America conference in Baltimore in August 2025.
