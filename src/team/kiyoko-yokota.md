I am a limnologist (lake scientist) who is particularly interested in the phytoplankton and aquatic stoichiometry. My colleagues and I maintain a high-frequency data buoy on Otsego Lake and other sensors and loggers that supplement the buoy data during winter and on the shore year-round. I partner with local municipalities and NGOs to share my research and expertise in local lake and watershed management efforts.
