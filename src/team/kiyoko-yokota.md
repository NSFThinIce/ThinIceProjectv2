## Biography

Dr. Kiyoko Yokota is a limnologist (lake scientist) who is particularly interested in the phytoplankton and aquatic stoichiometry. 

## Research Focus

She and her colleagues maintain a high-frequency data buoy on Otsego Lake and other sensors and loggers that supplement the buoy data during winter and on the shore year-round. She partners with local municipalities and NGOs to share her research and expertise in local lake and watershed management efforts.
