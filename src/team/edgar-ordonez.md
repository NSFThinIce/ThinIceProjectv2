## Biography
Edgar Ordonez is currently attending SUNY New Paltz to obtain a bachelor’s degree in Organismal Biology. Formerly, he worked as a Summer Research Technician in Dr. Richardson’s lab, where he calibrated different measuring sensors for the SUNY New Paltz lab's weekly water sampling at Mohonk lake, while gaining experience in data management and laboratory techniques.

## Research Focus
During the summer of 2025, he was given the opportunity to participate in a research project, titled “*The Effects of Ice Coverage on the Abundance of Algae”* with the help of his lab partner Gabriella Small and their advisor Dr. Richardson.
