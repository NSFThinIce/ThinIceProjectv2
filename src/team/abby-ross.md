## Biography

I am a microbiologist and biogeochemist broadly interested in how aquatic microorganisms shape and are shaped by their environment. My research projects range from understanding microbial taxonomic and functional diversity in glacial vs snow-fed alpine streams to identifying drivers of algal biomass and cyanotoxin concentration in lakes across the US. When I’m not working with analytical instrumentation in the lab or looking at glaciers and algae in the field, I like to trail run, cook, read, and go on hikes to look at glaciers and algae.
