## Biography

Scott Higgins is a Research Scientist at the IISD-Experimental Lakes Area (IISD-ELA), which operates a whole-ecosystem research program based in the boreal ecoregion of Canada. As part of his responsibilities, he manages the long-term ecological research (LTER) program of the IISD-ELA, which is responsible for managing the infrastructure, personnel and data collection for meteorology, hydrology, and lake sampling.  

## Research Focus

Scott Higgins is an active researcher and his primary research interests focus on algal ecology, climate change, and contaminant impacts on freshwater ecosystems.
