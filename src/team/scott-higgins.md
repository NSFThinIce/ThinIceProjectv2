I am a Research Scientist at the IISD-Experimental Lakes Area (IISD-ELA), which operates a whole-ecosystem research program based in the boreal ecoregion of Canada. As part of my responsibilities, I manage the long-term ecological research (LTER) program of the IISD-ELA, which is responsible for managing the infrastructure, personnel and data collection for meteorology, hydrology, and lake sampling.  I an active researcher and my primary research interests focus on algal ecology, climate change and contaminant impacts on freshwater ecosystems.
