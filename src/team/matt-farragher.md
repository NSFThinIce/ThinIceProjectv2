Matt Farragher works at 7 Lakes Alliance, which is a regional conservation organization located in Belgrade, Maine. In his role as Lake Scientist, he coordinates water quality monitoring in the field and processing water samples in the lab. Their primary objective in collecting this data is to identify the causes of water quality decline and to inform management plans to improve or remediate their lakes. Great Pond and Salmon Lake are their two lakes included in the Thin Ice set of gradient lakes. By collaborating on this project, they hope to gain a better understanding of these lake's nutrients, algal communities, and under-ice dynamics.
