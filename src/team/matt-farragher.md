I work at 7 Lakes Alliance, which is a regional conservation organization located in Belgrade, Maine. In my role as Lake Scientist, I coordinate water quality monitoring in the field and processing water samples in the lab. Our primary objective in collecting this data is to identify the causes of water quality decline and to inform management plans to improve or remediate our lakes. Great Pond and Salmon Lake are our two lakes included in the Thin Ice set of gradient lakes. By collaborating on this project, we hope to gain a better understanding of these lake's nutrients, algal communities, and under-ice dynamics.
